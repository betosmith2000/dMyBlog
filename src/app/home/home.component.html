Features

Decentralized

Decentralized Apps are not controlled by any single authority. Users own their identities and data. No censorship.

Blockstack ID

Blockstack ID provides user-controlled login and storage that enable you to take back control of your identity and data. It's blockchain-based security and encryption.

Privacy

Private posts are encrypted with encryption keys that only you hold.

Simple & Easy to use

You just have to login and you already have your blog ready. Create posts quickly with the WYSIWYG text editor in a simple interface.

Ad free

Zero advertising! Your readers and you can enjoy a clean interface so you can focus on the most important, the content!

And more...
